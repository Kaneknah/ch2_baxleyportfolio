About Me

I am a former High School teacher who has recently transitioned to web design. Born and raised in San Diego, I attended San Diego State University for my BA in History. I also acquired a Masters in Education from Azusa Pacific University and a Clear Teaching Credential from University of California San Diego. I have been in the Educational Field in California and Montana for over 12 years, before I decided to change directions and pursue a career in web design.

I have(will soon) completed the Berkely Bootcamp in Full Stack Web Development in April and have provided several examples of my completed projects through that course. I have also provided some of my own projects that I have completed on my own. I currently live outside of Missoula Montana with my beautiful wife and my three daughters and son. I am very excited to combined my professional knowledge with my newly learned skills to dive into an engaging career in web design.
